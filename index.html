📱 Privacy Policy

National Exam Center - SSC 2026 Preparation App

📅 Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Welcome to National Exam Center ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

📋 1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address and password (when you register)
  • Usage Data: Study time, quiz scores, and progress tracking
  • Communications: Information you provide when contacting support

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Advertising ID: For serving personalized ads (guests only)
  • Network Information: IP address, network type
  • App Usage: Features used, time spent, crash reports

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain our educational services
  • Progress Tracking: To save your study progress and quiz results
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Advertising: To show relevant ads (guest users only)
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

📢 3. Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect and use data about you to provide personalized ads. Learn more at Google's Advertising Policy.

Guest Users Only

Ads are shown only to guest users. Registered (premium) users have an ad-free experience.

What AdMob Collects:

  • Advertising ID (for ad personalization)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Coarse location (if permitted)
  • Ad interactions (clicks, impressions)

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication: For user sign-in and account management (Google)
  • Firebase Firestore: For cloud data storage and sync (Google)
  • Google AdMob: For advertising (guest users only)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

🌍 5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Guest progress is stored only on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Registered user data is stored securely on Firebase servers
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Retention: We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services

🔐 6. Your Rights and Choices

For All Users:

  • Opt-out of Ads: You can disable personalized ads in your device settings (Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of interest-based ads)
  • Delete Guest Data: Uninstalling the app removes all guest data

For Registered Users:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update your information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion
  • Export: Download your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students preparing for SSC exams. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

🌐 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers operate (Google Cloud locations). By using our app, you consent to this transfer.
